About Us

Welcome to our Team-based To-Do App! This platform is designed to help you and your team stay organized, focused, and productive. Whether you're managing a small team project or coordinating tasks in a company, our intuitive interface makes it easy to track progress and collaborate efficiently.

How to get started:
1. Create an account or log in.
2. Set up your first team and invite members.
3. Start adding tasks, assigning priorities, and tracking progress together!
